Privacy Policy

We do not directly collect any personal data. However, our app uses third-party advertising SDKs, including Google AdMob and AppLovin. These services may automatically collect:

  • Device or other identifiers (such as Advertising ID, Android ID)

Purpose of Data Collection

  • To serve personalized and non-personalized ads

Data Security

All data transmitted is encrypted in transit. We do not sell user data.

User Choices

Users can reset or opt out of personalized ads by adjusting their device settings (Google Settings → Ads → Reset advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization).
