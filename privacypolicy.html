Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

This app STREAMBTW - NFL Live Stream provides NFL news, live scores, and fixtures. The app is free to use and does not require user login or account creation.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information such as name, email, or phone number. However, our app uses third-party SDKs and services (Google and AppLovin) that may automatically collect the following:

  • Device or other identifiers (Advertising ID, Android ID, Device model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (for ad relevance and analytics)
  • App usage data (ad interactions, screens visited, performance events)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics (for stability and debugging)
  • Push notification tokens (for sending notifications)

Purpose of Data Collection

  • To serve personalized and non-personalized ads
  • For analytics and performance measurement
  • For fraud prevention, security, and compliance
  • To send app notifications (via Firebase Cloud Messaging)

Data Security

All data transmitted is encrypted in transit. We do not sell user data to third parties.

User Choices

Users have control over some of the data collected:

  • You can reset or opt out of personalized ads from device settings: Google Settings → Ads → Reset advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • You can disable app notifications from your device’s system settings

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following services, which may collect and process data according to their own policies:

  • Google AdMob (Ads)
  • Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, FCM)
  • AppLovin (Ads)

Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
